We’re dedicated to you

At StepStone Behavior Support Corp, we believe every child deserves the opportunity to grow, communicate, and thrive. We provide quality ABA therapy for children with autism and developmental challenges.

Our team of certified professionals uses compassionate, individualized care to help children build new skills, reduce challenging behaviors, and reach meaningful milestones. We partner with families every step of the way.

Focuses on the Individual

ABA therapy at Stepstone is personalized and centered on each person’s unique needs. We start by understanding where you are and help you reach new milestones step by step.

Develops Essential Life Skills

ABA therapy builds practical skills, including communication, social interaction, self-care, and problem-solving, setting the stage for greater independence and success.

Builds Confidence

Each skill gained through ABA brings more than progress—it creates lasting confidence and motivation for individuals and their families.
