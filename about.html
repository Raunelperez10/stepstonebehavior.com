A Second Home for Your Children

At StepStone Behavior Support, we understand that every child deserves to feel safe, understood, and supported. That’s why we’ve created a second home—a place filled with care, patience, and encouragement. We work closely with families to help each child grow emotionally, socially, and academically. Here, your child is not just another client. They are family.

What is ABA Therapy?

ABA stands for Applied Behavior Analysis. It’s a proven therapy that helps children improve important life skills. We use ABA to help children learn to communicate, follow routines, express their feelings, and gain independence. Each child gets a personalized plan based on their needs. Our team is trained to turn small steps into big achievements. ABA is not just a therapy—it’s a path to brighter days.

We Welcome Every Child, Just as They Are

Every child is unique. Every story matters. At StepStone, we celebrate differences. We welcome all families, all backgrounds, and all abilities. We know that children learn in different ways, at different times—and we’re here to meet them exactly where they are. This is a place where joy, kindness, and progress go hand in hand.

Our mission is to empower children and families through compassionate, evidence-based therapy services that bring real results and lasting confidence.
